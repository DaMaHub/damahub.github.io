About

Components

DaMaHub builds on established standards and an emerging decentralised technology stack, complementing existing research infrastructure while bridging the semantic gap between e-infrastructure providers and domain specialists. The community governance provides for co-ordination that is lightweight but sufficient to ensure a minimal set of production quality, reliable network nodes.
